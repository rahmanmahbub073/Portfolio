About me

HEY! GLAD YOU'RE HERE. A LITTLE ABOUT ME...

Good Day! This is Md Mahbubur Rahman. I'm from Bangladesh. I want to introduce myself as a student in the major of Transportations and Communications Engineering at Chang'an University. I grew up in Noakhali but now live in Chittagong, Bangladesh. I have completed my bachelor’s degree from the Chongqing University of Science and technology, and my major was computer science technology.

My academic experience in research started to train a predictive model based on Machine Learning for Credit Card Fraud Detection. It focused on detecting fraudulent behaviors by using advanced machine learning techniques. And another one is my bachelor’s dissertation “Image content review system based on deep learning object identification”. It is a detection system developed by the deep learning algorithm. This system identifies unwanted images from real-time videos and a target detection algorithm was used to train the detection model.

I have almost two years of job experience in the different sector such as engineering and teaching. My strength is I’m a self-motivated and hardworking person. I love to learn innovative things. My long-term goal is to become a Ph.D. researcher. My hobbies are listening to music, reading books and photography.
